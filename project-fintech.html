FinTech Dashboard

Real-time data visualization for investment professionals — making complex financial data actionable at a glance.

Client Capital Insights
Timeline 4 Months
Role Data Visualization & Frontend

Overview

Capital Insights manages over $2 billion in assets. Their analysts were drowning in spreadsheets and switching between 6 different tools just to get a complete picture of their portfolio performance.

We built a unified dashboard that pulls data from multiple sources, processes it in real-time, and presents actionable insights through beautiful, interactive visualizations.

The Challenge

01

Data Fragmentation

Portfolio data scattered across Bloomberg terminals, internal databases, Excel files, and third-party APIs.

02

Real-Time Requirements

Market data needs to update in milliseconds. Any delay could mean missed opportunities or increased risk.

03

Complex Calculations

Risk metrics, portfolio attribution, and performance analytics require heavy computational processing.

04

User Diversity

From senior portfolio managers to junior analysts — each with different needs and technical comfort levels.

Our Approach

Data Architecture

Week 1-3

Designed a data pipeline that ingests from 12 different sources, normalizes the data, and stores it in a time-series optimized database.

User Research

Week 2-4

Shadowed analysts during their daily workflows to understand exactly what data they need, when they need it, and how they make decisions.

Visualization Design

Week 5-8

Created custom chart components optimized for financial data — candlesticks, heat maps, correlation matrices, and risk dashboards.

Real-Time Engine

Week 9-14

Built WebSocket connections for live market data, implemented efficient state management, and optimized rendering for 60fps animations.

Security & Compliance

Week 15-16

Implemented role-based access control, audit logging, and ensured compliance with financial industry regulations.

Key Features

Live Market Feed

Real-time price updates with sub-second latency. Custom alerts for price movements and volume spikes.

Risk Analytics

VaR calculations, stress testing scenarios, and exposure breakdowns updated in real-time.

Custom Dashboards

Drag-and-drop widget system lets each user build their perfect workspace with the metrics that matter.

Performance Attribution

Break down returns by sector, geography, and individual holdings to understand what's driving performance.

Collaborative Notes

Annotate charts, share insights with team members, and build an institutional knowledge base.

Export & Reports

One-click generation of client reports, regulatory filings, and custom PDF exports.
