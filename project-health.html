Health Tracker

A comprehensive mobile app helping users take control of their wellness journey — from daily habits to long-term health goals.

Client WellLife Inc.
Timeline 5 Months
Role Mobile Development & UI/UX

Overview

WellLife wanted to create more than just another fitness app. They envisioned a holistic health companion that tracks nutrition, exercise, sleep, mental wellness, and medical data — all in one beautiful, easy-to-use interface.

We built a cross-platform mobile app that syncs with wearables, integrates with healthcare providers, and uses smart algorithms to provide personalized health insights and recommendations.

The Challenge

01

Data Privacy

Health data is sensitive. We needed HIPAA-compliant architecture with end-to-end encryption and user-controlled data sharing.

02

Device Integration

Supporting Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, and dozens of other wearables with different APIs and data formats.

03

User Engagement

Most health apps are abandoned within a week. We needed to create habits, not just features.

04

Personalization

Health is personal. A 25-year-old athlete has different needs than a 60-year-old managing diabetes.

Our Approach

User Research

Week 1-3

Interviewed 50+ potential users across different age groups, health conditions, and fitness levels. Mapped their daily routines and pain points.

UX Design

Week 4-7

Designed an intuitive interface that surfaces the right information at the right time. Morning shows sleep quality, evening prompts for gratitude journaling.

Core App Development

Week 8-14

Built with React Native for cross-platform efficiency. Native modules for HealthKit and Google Fit integration.

AI & Personalization

Week 15-18

Developed machine learning models that analyze user patterns and provide personalized recommendations, meal suggestions, and workout plans.

Beta Testing & Launch

Week 19-20

500-user beta program to gather feedback, fix bugs, and optimize onboarding flow before public launch.

Key Features

Smart Dashboard

Context-aware home screen that adapts throughout the day. Morning shows sleep data, afternoon shows activity progress.

Nutrition Tracking

Barcode scanning, photo recognition for meals, and a database of 500,000+ foods with detailed macro and micronutrients.

Workout Library

300+ guided workouts from yoga to HIIT, with video demonstrations and adaptive difficulty based on fitness level.

Sleep Analysis

Deep sleep tracking with smart alarm that wakes you during light sleep phases for better mornings.

Mental Wellness

Guided meditations, breathing exercises, mood tracking, and gratitude journaling for holistic health.

Health Records

Store and share medical records, track medications, and sync with healthcare provider portals.
