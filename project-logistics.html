Smart Logistics

AI-powered routing system that optimizes delivery networks in real-time — saving fuel, time, and operational costs.

Client TransitFlow
Timeline 8 Months
Role AI/ML & Systems Architecture

Overview

TransitFlow operates a fleet of 2,000+ delivery vehicles across 15 cities. Their manual routing process was inefficient, leading to wasted fuel, late deliveries, and frustrated customers.

We built an intelligent logistics platform that uses machine learning to optimize routes in real-time, predict delivery windows, and adapt to changing conditions like traffic, weather, and driver availability.

The Challenge

01

Dynamic Variables

Routes change constantly — new orders come in, traffic conditions shift, drivers call in sick. Static planning doesn't work.

02

Scale

50,000+ deliveries per day across 2,000 vehicles. The optimization problem is computationally massive.

03

Delivery Windows

Customers expect accurate ETAs. A 2-hour delivery window isn't good enough anymore.

04

Cost Pressure

Fuel costs are rising. Every unnecessary kilometer costs money and increases carbon footprint.

Our Approach

Data Collection & Analysis

Week 1-4

Analyzed 2 years of historical delivery data — routes, times, fuel consumption, traffic patterns, and customer feedback.

ML Model Development

Week 5-12

Built predictive models for delivery time estimation, traffic forecasting, and demand prediction using gradient boosting and neural networks.

Optimization Engine

Week 13-20

Developed a constraint-based optimization system that considers vehicle capacity, driver hours, delivery windows, and real-time traffic.

Real-Time Integration

Week 21-28

Connected with GPS systems, traffic APIs, and order management to enable dynamic re-routing as conditions change.

Pilot & Rollout

Week 29-32

Started with one city, measured results, fine-tuned models, then rolled out to all 15 cities over 4 weeks.

Key Features

Dynamic Routing

Routes recalculate every 5 minutes based on real-time traffic, new orders, and changing conditions.

Predictive ETAs

Machine learning models predict delivery times with 95% accuracy within a 15-minute window.

Fleet Dashboard

Real-time visibility into every vehicle — location, status, next stops, and performance metrics.

Driver App

Turn-by-turn navigation, proof of delivery, customer communication, and performance gamification.

Demand Forecasting

Predict order volumes by area and time to pre-position vehicles and staff appropriately.

Carbon Tracking

Monitor and report CO2 emissions with optimization targets for sustainability goals.
